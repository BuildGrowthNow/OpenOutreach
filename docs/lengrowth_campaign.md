# Lengrowth Outbound Campaign

This document outlines the target customer and keyword strategy for acquiring new users for **LenGrowth**.

## What LenGrowth Does

LenGrowth is a growth operating system that helps teams turn ideas into actual progress.

It gets the context of your business, shows you what really matters today, and gives you the path to growth with the best wins first.

**What we help with:**
- Figuring out what to do next for your growth
- Turning good ideas into tasks people actually finish
- Keeping work visible so it does not get lost in Slack or half-finished docs
- Marketing ideas that work for your specific business
- Distribution strategies that match what you are building

**How it works:**
- We learn your business context first
- Then suggest the next move with clear reasoning
- Help you track the work and keep it in one place
- Give you reusable assets as you go

## Why It Matters

A lot of growing teams run into the same problem: too many good ideas, not enough clean follow-through.

They do not need more tools or more meetings. They need a cleaner way to turn their best ideas into work that actually moves the needle.

That is the gap LenGrowth fills.

## Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary Target: Growth Teams & Founders

Our ideal customer is the **"Growth Operator"** — a strategic growth leader who is data-driven, execution-focused, and building scalable growth systems.

**Key Characteristics:**

* **Strategic & Data-Driven**: They need a system that helps them understand what matters, decide what to do next, and execute the work with minimal friction.
* **Growth-Oriented**: They are focused on measurable business outcomes - pipeline, conversion, retention, and activation.
* **Tech-Savvy**: They work with modern tech stacks and understand the value of integrated systems (FastAPI, MongoDB, Next.js).
* **Scale-Focused**: They need a platform that scales with their business, from early-stage founder to enterprise teams.
* **AI-Adopting**: They see value in AI-assisted execution while maintaining human oversight and specialist support options.

### Secondary Target: Agencies & Consultancies

Agencies managing multiple clients need a centralized growth operating system to:
- Manage multiple client growth strategies
-_coordinate AI-assisted execution across accounts
- Track growth KPIs consistently
- Scale delivery without adding headcount

The follow-up agent should:

1. **Discovery Mode (Default)**:

   - Focus on strategic alignment, hybrid execution, and enterprise readiness
   - Highlight the three-tier execution model (Humans, AI Agents, Domain Experts)

   - Full stack platform (not just analytics)
   - Hybrid execution with AI AND human specialist options
   - Enterprise-grade security from the start
   - Designed for both founders and teams
   - Comprehensive from onboarding to execution

## Keywords to Avoid (Low Quality Targets)

- People at very large enterprises (>500 employees) unless they're in a startup division
- People at agencies that only offer outsource services (not strategy)
- People whose profiles indicate they're only looking for full-time jobs
- People at companies that are clearly not in growth mode

## Daily Limits & Pace

- Connection requests: 15-20 per day
- Follow-ups: 20-25 per day
- Initial outreach days between messages: 2-3 days
- Follow-up sequence: 3-5 messages before closing
- Wait for replies before sending more - quality over quantity
